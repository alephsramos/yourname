Movie Synopsis

Your Name (Kimi no Na wa) is a 2016 Japanese animated film directed by Makoto Shinkai. The story follows two teenagers, Taki, a high school boy living in Tokyo, and Mitsuha, a girl from a rural town, who inexplicably begin to swap bodies intermittently. As they wake up in each other’s lives without understanding why, they must navigate daily tasks, relationships, and the challenges of living in a body not their own. They communicate by leaving notes and messages for each other, gradually growing closer despite never having met in person. Taki and Mitsuha develop a deep bond as they try to uncover the mystery behind their strange connection. However, Taki soon discovers that Mitsuha’s town was destroyed by a comet years earlier, leading to a desperate race against time. He attempts to change fate and prevent the disaster from happening by reconnecting with Mitsuha and saving her and her town.
